About

Around March 2022, three attorneys—Kyle E. Mitchell, Matthew Olsman, and Noel “Kaison” Lawrence—met at an Alameda County Bar Association meeting on tech. They decided to create and share this guide to help their clients and the public understand key points about texting, chatting, and the law.
